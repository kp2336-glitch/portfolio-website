About

Nice to meet you

I'm a Marketing and Media Analytics professional in training, driven by a passion for transforming data into meaningful stories. With a foundation in English Literature, Cinema Studies, and entertainment business, I approach analytics with a storyteller’s mindset—connecting audience behavior, content, and performance data to uncover insights that matter. Today, I focus on marketing, media, and audience insights, with particular interest in the streaming, advertising, and digital marketing industries.

I’ve gained hands-on experience across the media and technology landscape: evaluating early-stage startups as an Open Innovation intern at CJ America Inc., analyzing multichannel performance and predictive revenue trends at Open Road Integrated Media, and applying data-driven problem solving through both the Roku x NYU SPS Datathon and the Salesforce Agentforce Hackathon. Across these experiences, I’ve developed a strong interest in streaming, advertising, and digital marketing—spaces where data, creativity, and audience understanding intersect.

Applied Technical Capstone Project: eBook Metadata Quality Scoring Model

Built an end-to-end Python-based metadata quality scoring model for a 100,000-title ebook catalog by aggregating metadata, traffic, promotions, and pricing data to prioritize titles for optimization.

The project addresses catalog performance at scale, giving Open Road a systematic, data-driven way to identify and act on metadata gaps through a Tableau decision dashboard.

Advanced Business Analytics Project

Developed an end-to-end churn retention strategy for a major telecom company using the CRISP-DM framework, combining predictive churn modeling in RapidMiner with Expected Value analysis to identify which at-risk customers are worth retaining.

By layering sentiment and textual analysis, the project validated two distinct behavioral churn segments and produced a prioritized, scalable deployment roadmap grounded in statistical modeling and business value.
